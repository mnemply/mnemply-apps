# Mnemply Bingo - Official Rules (Expansion)

Mnemply Bingo Expansion is a fun way to practise the Expansion Mem-Links using the Mnemply number tiles. Players draw a Cousin Tile and a Mem-Link Tile, use the Flip-It Rule when needed, reveal the matching Mem-Link, and mark it on their Bingo card.

## What You Need

- Mnemply Bingo Expansion cards
- The Mnemply Bingo Expansion app
- A marker, counter, or pencil for each player

## Game Setup

1. Give each player one Expansion Bingo card.
2. Open the Expansion Bingo app.
3. Choose the winning pattern before the game begins.
4. Press New Game if you are starting a fresh round.

## How to Play

1. Press Draw Tiles.
2. Look at the two number tiles:
   - Cousin Tile: the blue tile.
   - Mem-Link Tile: the green tile.
3. Check the Flip-It Rule.
4. If the Mem-Link Tile number is larger than the Cousin Tile number, press Flip-It.
5. Once the Cousin Tile is the larger or equal number, press Reveal Mem-Link.
6. Players look for that Mem-Link on their Bingo card.
7. If the Mem-Link appears on their card, they mark the space.
8. Continue drawing until someone completes the chosen winning pattern.

## The Flip-It Rule

The Cousin Tile must be the larger or equal number.

Examples:

- 3 x 7 becomes 7 x 3.
- 4 x 9 becomes 9 x 4.
- 8 x 8 stays 8 x 8.

In the app, Reveal Mem-Link will not work if the Mem-Link Tile is larger than the Cousin Tile. Press Flip-It first, then reveal.

## Bingo Calling Rule

Each Expansion Mem-Link can only be called once in a game. When all Expansion Mem-Links have been called, start a New Game.

## Winning Patterns

Choose one pattern before the game begins:

- Classic Bingo: complete any row, column, or diagonal. There are multiple row, column, and diagonal examples for Expansion cards.
- X Pattern: complete both diagonals.
- Z Pattern: complete the top row, one diagonal, and the bottom row.
- O Pattern: complete all outside squares.
- Full Card: mark every space on the Bingo card.

Pattern image examples are stored in `assets/rules/winning patterns/expansion`.

## Optional Gameplay Variations

- Speed Round: players must find and mark the Mem-Link within 5 seconds.
- Team Play: players work in pairs and share a card.
- Say It Out Loud: players say the Cousin, Mem-Link Position, and Mem-Link before marking.
- Memory Check: after revealing, ask players to decode the answer using the Secret Code.

## Game Tips

- Encourage children to say the Mem-Link out loud when they mark it.
- Use Flip-It as a discovery moment, not a correction.
- Ask, "Which Cousin has this Mem-Link Position?"
- Remind players that Expansion uses Cousins 1 to 12.
- Keep the tone playful. The goal is confident recall, not speed pressure.

Have fun playing Mnemply Bingo Expansion.
