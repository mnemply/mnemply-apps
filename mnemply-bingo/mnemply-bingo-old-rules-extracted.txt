Mnemply Bingo - O(cid:15)icial Rules (Level 1)
Welcome to Mnemply Bingo (Level 1), a fun and interactive way to reinforce mnemonic learning
using Mnemply's system! This game helps players identify the correct mnemonics based on dice
rolls, strengthening memory and recognition skills.
How to Play
1. Setup
• Each player gets a Mnemply Bingo Card (Level 1).
• Use the special Mnemply dice:
o Two D6 dice (one for Cousins, one for Mnemonics).
2. Rolling the Dice
• The first die (Cousins Dice) determines which cousin is in play.
• The second die (Mnemonic Dice) determines the mnemonic associated with that cousin.
3. Finding the Mnemonic
• Players look for the mnemonic rolled on their Bingo Card.
• If the mnemonic appears, they mark the space.
• If the mnemonic is not on their card, they do nothing and wait for the next roll.
4. Winning the Game
Players can win in di)erent ways depending on the variation being played:

Optional Gameplay Variations
• Speed Round → Players must find and mark their mnemonic within 5 seconds of rolling!
• Team Play → Players work in pairs, taking turns rolling and marking their shared card.
• Last Mnemonic Standing → The last player with at least one unmarked space wins.
Game Tips
• Encourage kids to say the mnemonic out loud when marking their card.
• Use this game to spark discussions about how mnemonics help with memory recall.
• Make it extra fun by awarding small prizes for winners!
